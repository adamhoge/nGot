Simple tetris like game with power ups
can chain power ups
4 types of power ups
vertical line
standard horizontal line
radius (maybe 3 tiles)
reverse gravity?
gravity increases every 30 seconds
tiles are worth 1 point each
multipliers are given for each additional line
multipliers are given for each power up
gravity increases variably depending on average points per second
the overall multiplier increases everytime gravity increases
there is only 1 level
when a row is removed with a power up, it activates
